Sushant Singh Rajput’s demise comes amid recent deaths of many Bollywood celebrities, including Irrfan Khan, Rishi Kapoor and Wajid Khan of Sajid-Wajid duo and director Basu Chatterjee. Twitter/@itsSSR

Rajput, best known for playing former India captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni in his biopic M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story, was found hanging at his Bandra residence, police said.
