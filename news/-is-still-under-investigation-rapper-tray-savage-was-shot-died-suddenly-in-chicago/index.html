HIP-HOP Rapper Tray Savage Dies at 26

Chief Keef’s record label signee, Tray Savage, died on Friday (June 19), Billboard can confirm. He was 26 years old.

According to TMZ, who first reported the news, Tray (born Kentray Young) was shot and killed while driving around on the South Side of Chicago. He was rushed to the University of Chicago Medical Canter, where he was later pronounced dead.

“The entire Chief Glo Gang Family are extremely saddened about the loss of Kentray ‘Tray Savage’ Young who was taken too soon today,” a representative said in a statement. “His first love was always devoted to his fiancé, kids, family, and music. Regrettably, we can’t take back the actions done by others, but we stand firmly behind the fact that Kentray ‘Tray Savage’ Young should be here today alive and continuing his amazing contributions to society. His friends, family and entertainment family will continue to honor his legacy.”

Young was signed to Glory Boyz Entertainment label, founded by Fredo Santana and Chief Keef.

He was known for songs like “Faces,” “Know Who I Am,” and “Got The Mac.” In 2013, he teamed up with Chief Keef for “Chiefin Keef.”

June 20 (UPI) – Kentray Young, the hip-hop artist who went by the name Tray Savage, was shot and killed in Chicago, police said.

Young, 26, was driving Friday morning when an unknown assailant fired at his vehicle, striking him in the neck and shoulder, and causing him to crash into three other cars.

“Le rappeur américain Tray Savage, 26 ans, abattu à Chicago”

Le monde du rap est en deuil : Tray Savage est mort ce vendredi 19 juin. Selon la police, le rappeur de 26 ans venait de déposer sa petite amie dans le quartier de Chatham dans le sud de Chicago où il vivait, lorsque des coups de feu ont retenti. Touché au cou et à l’épaule, l’interprète de My Set aurait continué de conduire quelques mètres avant de percuter plusieurs voitures selon le site Chigago Suntimes. Secouru par sa petite amie, qui s’est précipitée pour l’extraire du véhicule, Tray Savage a ensuite été emmené au centre médical universitaire de Chicago où il a été déclaré mort.

Pour le moment aucun suspect n’a été arrêté, l’assaillant ayant pris la fuite. Cependant plusieurs témoins ont rapporté à la police locale avoir aperçu un SUV blanc au moment de la fusillade. Une enquête pour homicide est encore en cours. Un drame qui a beaucoup attristé le monde du rap et de la musique qui lui on rendu hommage depuis l’annonce de son décès.

Un autre rappeur tué par balles
