But it was also, apparently, ripe for memes. There's a new meme in town, born from a simple frame from the movie. Here, demonstrated, is the proper way to go about using it. Mostly, you'll see, meme-makers like to label Green Goblin as a person or entity (or idea) that's going to take Spidey by surprise.
