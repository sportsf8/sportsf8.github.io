Watch Lil Yachty, Drake, and DaBaby's New Video for 'Oprah's Bank Account'

After a number of teases, Lil Yachty has delivered "Oprah's Bank Account" featuring Drake and DaBaby. Yachty first revealed plans to release Lil Boat 3 last year, but now the release is finally on the horizon with the arrival of his star-studded new single. With the type of bouncy production the Atlanta rapper made his name for, courtesy of Earl on the Beat, it's clear he's having a lot of fun on "Oprah's Bank Account." The new track is accompanied by a Director X video, which sees Yachty—who goes by Boprah—goofing off and offering his best impression of Oprah Winfrey. It also features him doing interviews with Drake and DaBaby while getting off an Oprah outfit or two. Perhaps the best moments come when Drizzy takes a moment to playfully make fun of himself, although he also jokingly refers to Yachty's music as "nursery rhymes that nobody can understand." Lil Yachty spoke with Complex about "Oprah's Bank Account," including how he got Drake and DaBaby on the song, and how he originally wanted Lizzo on it. You can read the full interview here. At the beginning of 2020, Yachty teased his fans with a preview of new music. "Y'all mind if I treat 2020 like 2016 with the melodies?" he wrote alongside a video of him in the studio listening to "Oprah's Bank Account." The song will turn up on the long-awaited Lil Boat 3, which follows the release of his 2018 album Nuthin' 2 Prove. Watch the video for "Oprah's Bank Account" above.
