WWE Monday Night RAW Live Coverage: Edge Returns, Drew McIntyre In Action, Shayna Baszler

Welcome to Wrestling Inc.'s live coverage of WWE RAW from Capital One Arena in Washington, D.C. - Recap of Randy Orton taking out Edge two months ago. Edge returns tonight. - We see a shot out back as we wait for Edge's arrival to the building. - WWE RAW Women's Champion Becky Lynch heads out to the ring. She will face Shayna Baszler at WrestleMania. Lynch grabs a mic and says the time for joking is over and she finally knows who her opponent is, the cage fighter, Shayna Baszler. We see highlights from Baszler's dominate victory at last night's PPV. Baszler says she's taking the title and there's not a damn thing Lynch can do about it. Back to the ring, Lynch recognizes Baszler cut through everyone in last night's match, even Asuka, who it took Lynch her entire career to beat. Lynch calls Baszler a "black hole of charisma" and "I may be a b----, but I'm the b---- who runs this division." Lynch recalls Baszler not shaking her hand after last year's WrestleMania and says Baszler is the bigger threat to come here way since she pried the title off her friends, Ronda Rousey. Lynch says Baszler has been hiding in the shadows, looking to rewrite history. Believes Baszler is trying to discount Lynch and she's going to smash Baszler's face for it, throwing down the mic afterwards. - Rey Mysterio makes his way to the ring for his match against Angel Garza. Rey Mysterio vs. Angel Garza with Zelina Vega Garza taking his time getting into the match as he poses on the top turnbuckle. Mysterio runs right in at him, hurricanrana, sending Garza into the second turnbuckle. Garza with a wristlock, tries for another hurricanrana, blocked, and tossed away. Garza with a boot to the face, Mysterio with some back elbows, heads to the corner and tries to leap back over Garza and eats a kick right to the head. Another dropkick sends Mysterio out to the floor. Vega on the apron as Garza does the pants tearaway spot. Garza continues to beat up Mysterio as we go to split-screen break. Back in the ring, Garza pops Mysterio up and lands a big knee. Garza gets tossed out to the floor, Mysterio does the slide splash out on his opponent. Garza sent back into the ring, Mysterio with a springboard seated senton, running senton, kick to the head, cover, two-count. Garza up and hits a slingshot reverse suplex on Mysterio, cover for two. Mysterio looks for the 619, Garza ducks and pops him with a superkick. The two end up on the top turnbuckle, Mysterio fights Garza off, looks for the canadian destroy, no, but sets him up for the 619 and hits it. Mysterio with the springboard crossbody splash for the 1-2-3! Mysterio slipped a bit on that and landed somewhat hard on Garza. Winner: Rey Mysterio via Pinfall - Earlier today, Kevin Owens strolls into the building for tonight's show. He runs into AOP, Murphy, and Seth Rollins. Rollins chuckles as he eats some popcorn. He offers Owens some popcorn. Owens knows what's up and throws his suitcase at AOP, but they immediately attack him. Rollins with a stomp. AOP sends him into some metal cases. Murphy throws some more equipment on him. WWE officials show up to get them away from Owens. - Steve Austin will be on next Monday's RAW for March 16 (or 3/16). - Video package shown of NXT Women's Champion Rhea Ripley talking about her road to WrestleMania as she checks out Raymond James Stadium. Ripley will defend her title against Charlotte at WWE's biggest show of the year. Ripley noted when she first came to NXT, everybody said she looked like Charlotte (or a "Mini-Charlotte") so she wanted to make sure she showed everyone who she really is. - Charlotte's music hits and she strolls out to the ring, not in her usual ring gear. Charlotte takes the mic, and tells the crowd to meet Rhea Ripley, the next big thing, the new shiny toy. She says Ripley said to be the man you have to beat the man..."You are not me, you will never be me, there is only one me." Charlotte tells Ripley to enjoy the hype surrounding her because it will not last. Charlotte is challenging for the NXT Women's Championship because she wants to challenge for the title. Charlotte says it's one thing to stand in an empty stadium, and be in front of 90,000 people, only to be humbled by The Queen. Ripley rolls out with a smile on her face.
