Ricky Gervais mourning the death of his beloved cat, Ollie

Ricky Gervais is mourning the loss of a furry friend. The comedian shared Tuesday that his beloved cat, Ollie, has died a few months shy of her 17th birthday. YOUTUBE Ricky Gervais was first gifted Ollie on TV in 2003. This week, he's had to farewell his furry friend. "Just had to say goodbye to the sweetest little soul I've ever known," Gervais tweeted, along with two photos of Ollie. Gervais, 58, had often shared photos of the feline on his social media pages, and a separate Twitter account with the handle @myleftfang was devoted entirely to Ollie.
