Harry Styles bringing Love On Tour to New Zealand in November

The One Direction star will play Spark Arena on November 23, and will also be heading to Sydney, Brisbane , Melbourne and Perth. Styles will perform hits from his second solo album Fine Line, which features singles 'Adore You' and 'Watermelon Sugar'. It will be the British singer's first arena shows since his last tour, which included one New Zealand date in 2018. Styles will be joined by 22-year-old popstar Mabel, who has quickly become one of the biggest artists in the UK charts currently. Having shot to fame as part of boy band One Direction, which was formed on The X Factor UK, Styles launched his solo career after the band broke up in 2016. His latest album Fine Line broke the record as the biggest sales debut from a British male artist in the US.
