Corey Feldman Doc ‘(My Truth)’ Accuses Charlie Sheen Of Raping Corey Haim

Corey Feldman is making serious allegations in his new documentary. It wasn’t like ‘Oh, by the way, this happened.’ He went into great detail,” Feldman says of Haim in the doc, according to Entertainment Weekly.
