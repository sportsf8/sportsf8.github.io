(CNN) Chiranjeevi Sarja, a famous Indian actor, has passed away at the age of 39.

Sarja, who starred in 20 films including the popular “Amma I Love You,” died of a heart attack, according to B.S. Yediyurappa, chief minister of Karnataka.

“It is a shock to hear the news that famous Kannada artist, Shri Chiranjeevi Sarja, died of a heart attack. Premature death at an age of just 39 years is shocking. My prayers are with the family, may God give them the strength to endure this grief,” Yediyurappa tweeted on Sunday.

Sarja, who also starred in “Whistle,” and “Varadanayaka,” came from a family of actors. He is the nephew of Tamil actor Arjun Sarja and grandson of veteran actor Shakti Prasad, according to the Hindustan Times. He married actress Meghana Raj in 2018.

Following the news of his death, many of Sarja’s fans began posting their condolences on his last Instagram post , which featured his brother, Dhruva Sarja, who is also a famous Kannada actor.

“Chiranjeevi Sarja dead: Bollywood actor dies aged 39 as co-stars pay tribute”

Bollywood star Chiranjeevi Sarja has died of a heart attack at the age of 39.

The Indian actor was taken to a private hospital in Bengaluru, India on Sunday after complaining of severe chest pains and breathlessness.

Medical staff were unable to revive him and he died soon afterwards.

Chiranjeevi was best known for playing leading roles in 20 Bollywood movies, including 2018’s Amma I Love You.

He is survived by his actress wife of two years Meghana Raj, who he dated for years before they married in 2018.

(Image: Instagram)

A prolific actor, who had three films coming out this year alone, he shot to fame after appearing in 2009 movie, Vayuputra.

He added to his credentials with critically acclaimed performances in Whistle and Amma I Love You.

Chiranjeevi’s passing was confirmed in a tweet from The chief minister of Karnataka, India, BS Yediyurappa.

He wrote on Monday morning: “It is a shock to hear the news that Kannada famous artist Shri Chiranjeevi Sarja died of a heart attack.

“Premature death at an age of just 39 years is shocking. My prayers are with the family, may God give them the strength to endure this grief.” (Translated)

Yatheesh Govindaiah, the unit head at Apollo Hospitals Jayanagar also released a statement in which he detailed the actions taken by medical staff in an attempt to save Chiranjeevi.

(Image: Instagram)

He said: “Sarja was brought to our hospital [Apollo] in an unresponsive state. Attempts to revive him failed and he was declared dead by the doctors.

“On examination, Sarja was found unresponsive with an absent carotid pulse, cold peripheries and bilaterally dilated pupils.

“Hence cardio-pulmonary resuscitation was started according to the latest advanced cardiac life support protocol.”

Fellow Bollywood stars shared tributes to Chiranjeevi, with actor Sanjjanaa Galrani tweeting that he was in an “immense shocking state of mind.”

She said: “Cannot believe and I’m in immense shocking state of mind to know that Chiranjeevi Sarja is no more, very very saddening, my condolences to his family and [Meghna Raj] at this time of pain and sorrow.

“Cannot yet digest this fact, my hands are shivering as I write this msg .V v sad.”

Actress Priyamani added on Twitter : “Shocked to hear about #chiranjevvisarja’s demise!!! Can never forget his smiling face.”
