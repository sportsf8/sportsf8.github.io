Harry Styles to bring Love On Tour to Australia in November

Pop phenomenon Harry Styles will return to Australia in November for a series of arena shows celebrating his second hit album Fine Line. The former member of One Direction will perform at Sydney's Qudos Bank Arena on November 20, Brisbane's Entertainment Centre on November 26, Melbourne's Rod Laver Arena on November 28, and Perth's RAC Arena on December 2. Harry Styles in touring Australia. Credit:Tim Walker The local shows – which had been mysteriously teased all week on website tasteslikestrawberries.com, a line from Styles' recent chart-topping single Watermelon Sugar – are new additions to the 26-year-old's epic Love On Tour dates which launch in his Birmingham hometown in the UK on April 15.
