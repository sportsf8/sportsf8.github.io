Willem DaThicc indeed. The year was 2002. Spider-Man was released in cinemas and Willem Dafoe’s extremely fat ass graced our screens. As Spider-Man’s archnemesis, a huge, destructive superhuman showdown where lots of civilians were at risk — but none ever actually die — was a given. For Green Goblin, this happened when he attacked a bunch of New Yorkers during a festival in Times Square. Of course, ol’ trusty Spider-Man managed to save everyone, but the scene was great for a number of reasons. First we had MJ’s absolutely useless attempt to get up and save herself and then we had the kid who was just so ready to get crushed by the falling debris. However, the best shot was easily the one of Green Goblin’s fat booty on his glider, chasing Spider-Man as he ran down the street. The shot was so memorable that @rottingvale‘s tweet of the scene went viral.
