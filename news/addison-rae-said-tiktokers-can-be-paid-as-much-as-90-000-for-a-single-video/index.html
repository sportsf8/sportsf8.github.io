TikTok star Addison Rae shocked David Dobrik and Jason Nash with how much you can earn from one post on the platform.

Rae appeared on the podcast “VIEWS with David Dobrik and Jason Nash” to discuss how to make money on TikTok.

She said the most she’s heard someone has made from a single TikTok is $90,000.

Madison Beer and Dobrik then revealed how much they’ve known people to earn on Instagram and YouTube — upwards of $250,000 and $500,000 respectively.

Visit Insider’s homepage for more stories.

Addison Easterling, better known as Addison Rae, appeared on David Dobrik and Jason Nash’s podcast “VIEWS” this week to talk all things TikTok. Dobrik asked her how much a TikTok star can make from one post alone.

“One TikTok, that I’m aware of …” she began, before the others started to guess. In the end, she said she’d heard of one star on the platform being paid $90,000 for one sponsored video.

Dobrik said it’s a misconception that people get paid per post on TikTok.

“It’s only when it’s branded that you make money,” he said.

Madison Beer, who was also on the podcast, said TikTok is still relatively new so brands are still working it out. On a platform like Instagram, she said, stars are often paid upwards of $250,000.

“Instagram is insane,” she said. “People get paid like a million dollars for a story, some people.”

Dobrik then revealed how much he’s known one video make on YouTube.

“It’s like $500,000 for an integration on YouTube,” he said. “For like a 20-30 second plug, I’d say.”

“Addison Rae Breaks Down TikTok Hits”

“Not only does it really bring songs to the top of the charts, but obviously artists are really enjoying being on the app and bringing their music to the platform, and I think there’s no better way to do that than incorporate the app into their music,” she said.
