Peter Weberis ready to start a new chapter in his life with Hannah Ann Sluss by his side. During the Bachelor finale, the pilot popped the big question to none other than Hannah Ann, after Madison Prewitt decided Peter wasn't the man for her. She made her dramatic decision to leave just days before Peter would reveal the woman he wanted to spend the rest of his life with. Before he got down on one knee, however, he FaceTimed Hannah Ann's dad to ask for her hand in marriage. Despite her father's previous apprehension, he gave an enthusiastic yes.
