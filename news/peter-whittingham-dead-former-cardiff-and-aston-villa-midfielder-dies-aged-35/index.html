Peter Whittingham dead: Former Cardiff and Aston Villa midfielder dies aged 35

Peter Whittingham, 35, has died after suffering an accidental head injury. The former Cardiff and Aston Villa midfielder has been confirmed dead this afternoon after police reported he had suffered a head injury in a licensed area on March 7.

Whittingham was being treated in intensive care in Cardiff’s Heath Hospital before his passing. He leaves behind a wife, Amanda, who is currently pregnant, and a young son. Whittingham scored 85 goals for Cardiff over a 10-year spell at the club having made 413 appearances. He also played for Aston Villa between 2003-07 and made 21 appearances for England’s Under-21 side. Whittingham retired after a spell with Blackburn Rovers in 2018. “It is with an immeasurable amount of sorrow that we must inform supporters that Peter Whittingham has passed away at the age of 35,” a statement from Cardiff read.
