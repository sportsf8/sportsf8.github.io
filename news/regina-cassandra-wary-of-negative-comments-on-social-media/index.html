Regina Cassandra wary of negative comments on social media

Regina Cassandra is a hot favourite, not just among filmmakers, but also on social media. While every post of hers gets maximum traction, interestingly, the actress says she is ‘not so comfortable’ when it comes to interacting with people on social media.

“It’s not like I don’t want to interact with people; I’d love to socialise with fans on social media, but there’s so much cyber bullying out there that affects me. For me, my mental peace is very important,” explains Regina.

While asserting that she has always received positive comments regarding her performance, the actress however stresses that negative feedback and social media bullying do have an effect.

“If I post a photo, it gets 10k positive comments, and then there’s one negative comment that may affect me. So I don’t want to put myself in such a position because if I start reading those negative comments, I might get more analytical and start being too introspective,” the Jyo Achyutananda star.

The actress further says she likes the bygone era. “Back then there was no social media and there was a mystery that surrounded the actors. I like to have that mysterious disconnect,” she signs off.
