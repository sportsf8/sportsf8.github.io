Cricket Match Predictions: UVA T20 Premier League 2020 - Monaragala Hornets vs Wellawaya Vipers - Match 1

©UVA T20 Premier League 2020

The 1st match of the UVA T20 Premier League 2020 will be played between Monaragala Hornets and Wellawaya Vipers in Sri Lanka.

That Tilakratne Dilshan has agreed to be a part of Monaragala Hornets will go a long way in increasing the popularity of the UVA T20 Premier League. The batsman is one of the most celebrated Sri Lankan players, particularly in white-ball cricket.

The opening batsman has had many great outings for the side but none more so than his utter dominance in the 2009 T20 World Cup where he played a major role in guiding Sri Lanka to the final. Unfortunately, the team lost to Pakistan by 8 wickets but the opener ended as the Player of the Series.

Dilshan struck 317 runs in the T20 WC, the most in the tournament, from 7 matches at an average of 52.83 and a strike rate of almost 145. He has given a lot to the game, including the the famed Dilscoop and his presence will mean a lot to the side.

Hornets’ wicketkeeper Ajith Ekanayake is 54 years old but the love for cricket has kept him going. The wicketkeeper has played 9 T20 matches and has the highest of 27*.

Sachithra Serasinghe has 41 wickets from 37 T20 matches and will be the leading bowler for the Monaragala Hornets. All-rounder Umesh Karunaratne is another player to look out for. He has played 19 T20 matches in which has scored 266 runs and picked up 16 wickets.

Their opponents, Wellawaya Vipers have Ajantha Mendis as their skipper. Mendis last played competitive cricket for the Sri Lanka Legends, alongside Dilshan and Tushara, as late as 10 March, 2020.

Mendis may have ceased to be effective in international cricket but his bagful of variations will still be too much to handle for the Monaragala Hornets batsmen.

Cricket Betting Tips and Fantasy Cricket Match Predictions: UVA T20 Premier League 2020 - Unilions Mahiyanganaya vs Badulla Sea Eagles - Match 2

©UVA T20 Premier League 2020

Match 2 of the UVA T20 Premier League 2020 is between Unilions Mahiyanganaya and Badulla Sea Eagles, in Sri Lanka.

Thilan Tushara has been signed as the icon player by Unilions Mahiyanganaya. The bowler has played 53 T20 games from which he has picked up 71 wickets at an average of 19.29 and an economy of 7.42.

Tushara has the best of 3/8 and last played for Sri Lanka Legends in the Road Safety World Series in March this year.

Sriyaan Chandimal has been announced as the vice-captain of the side while Kavidu Gunarathne will don the wicket-keeping gloves.

For Badulla Sea Eagles, Farveez Maharoof will be seen in action. Maharoof has been announced as the skipper and the icon player of the side.

The former Sri Lankan international has played 99 T20 matches apart from representing Sri Lanka in 22 Tests, 109 ODIs and eight T20 Internationals. The bowler has picked up 94 T20 wickets at an average of 23.54 and an economy of 7.31.

While Maharoof can turn out to be too hot to handle for the batsmen, he can also handle himself with the bat and has the highest T20 score of 55*. Maharoof has a T20 batting average of 17.88 and a strike rate of 117.91.

Dasun Shanaka has also been roped in by the Sea Eagles. Shanaka is a quality all-rounder and has an average of 27.14 while striking at close to 145 from 93 T20 matches, in which he has scored 1846 runs.

With the ball as well, the all-rounder has done a decent job of picking up 23 wickets at an economy rate of 8.89.
