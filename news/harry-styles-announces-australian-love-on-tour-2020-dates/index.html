Harry Styles announces Australian Love On Tour 2020 dates

British-born superstars including Harry Styles, Stormzy and Mabel lead the nominations at this year’s BRIT Awards, taking place in London today. Harry Styles is coming to Australia. The former One Direction singer is bringing is Love On Tour show to Australia in November/December with special guest Mabel. Heading to Auckland, Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne and Perth, these will be Styles’ first arena shows since his massive 2018 Australian tour. Tickets for all shows go on sale at 2pm Friday, March 20. For tour, ticket and VIP Experience information, you can visit hstyles.co.uk and livenation.com.au. According to Live Nation, Love On Tour is in celebration of Styles’ second solo #1 album, Fine Line. Home to smash singles Lights up, Adore You, and Watermelon Sugar, the album peaked at #1 around the world including Australia and New Zealand. It debuted at #1 on the US’ Billboard 200 and entered into the record books as the biggest sales week for a UK male artist’s album since Nielsen Music began electronically tracking sales data in 1991. Harry Styles Love On Tour dates: Qudos Bank Arena, Sydney - November 20 Brisbane Entertainment Centre - November 26 Rod Laver Arena, Melbourne - November 28 RAC Arena, Perth - December 2
