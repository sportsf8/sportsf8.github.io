Harry Styles Has Announced An Australian Tour And Everybody Calm Down

IT'S HAPPENING. Beloved popstar Harry Styles has announced a string of Australian tour dates. The ‘Adore You’ singer will be heading our way in November, playing arenas in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Perth (sorry Adelaide). He’ll be coming armed with his new — and thoroughly excellent — album, Fine Line. He’ll be joined on all dates by UK singer Mabel. Eagle-eyed fans already had an inkling this was coming: on Sunday, promoter Live Nation teased the announcement by launching the website Tastes Like Strawberries — a phrase taken from his track ‘Watermelon Sugar’ — on Sunday. Fans promptly swarmed the website — Live Nation says it received more than 300,000 visitors within 24 hours. Tickets go on sale at 2pm on Friday, March 20. There are a few pre-sales happening before then: the AMEX card members pre-sale is happening from Tuesday March 17 at 1pm, and the My Live Nation pre-sale will kick off March 19 at 1pm. So prepare yourself accordingly. Check out all dates below, and for all ticketing information head to Live Nation’s website. Harry Styles Australian Tour Dates 2020 Friday November 20 — Qudos Bank Arena, Sydney Thursday November 26 — Brisbane Entertainment Centre, Brisbane Saturday November 28 — Rod Laver Arena, Melbourne Wednesday December 2 — RAC Arena, Perth
