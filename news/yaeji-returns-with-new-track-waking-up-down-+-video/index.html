Yaeji Returns With New Track 'Waking Up Down' + Video

NYC-via-Seoul electronic artist Yaeji made significant waves in 2017 with seemingly ubiquitous club fave 'raingurl', and now bounds into 2020 with 'Waking Up Down', the first taste of a new mixtape named WHAT WE DREW 우리가 그려왔던 set to drop in early April. Accompanied by a fantastically swish animated video created by the artist in collaboration with director Annie Xing Zhao and Studio Yotta, 'Waking Up Down' is a gently upbeat encouragement to master that daily routine, even when it feels like the most basic tasks can be a bit much. Let 'Waking Up Down' help you with your hump day and stay tuned for more from Yaeji in the near future... 'WHAT WE DREW 우리가 그려왔던' releases on Thursday 2nd April via XL Recordings / Rhythmethod.
