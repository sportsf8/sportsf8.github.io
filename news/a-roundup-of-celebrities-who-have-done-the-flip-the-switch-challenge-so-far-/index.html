A Roundup of Celebrities Who Have Done the 'Flip the Switch' Challenge (So Far)

Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez attend the 2019 Vanity Fair Oscar Party Hosted By Radhika Jones at Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts on Feb. 24, 2019 in Beverly Hills, Calif. The latest trend to hit TikTok is the #FlipTheSwitch challenge, in which users create videos flipping their light switches to the tune of Drake's 2018 single "Nonstop." When Drizzy raps "I just flip the switch," TikTokers dance, swap clothes, or go wild upon literally flipping the switch. A number of celebrities have gotten in on the trend, from Jennifer Lopez to Camila Cabello, creating hilarious videos on the social media platform. See all the celebs who have "flipped the switch" so far below. Jennifer Lopez & Alex Rodriguez A-Rod and Lopez stood in front of a mirror while the singer moved her hips seductively to "Nonstop." When they "flipped the switch," the lights went off, and the soon-to-be-married couple switched clothes. JLo was now holding the phone in A-Rod's suit and sunglasses, while the former MLB star rocked Lopez's white curve-hugging dress and gold hoops. Camila Cabello In the "Havana" singer's take on the trend, she switches clothes with her choreographer Calvit Jr. She "flips" into his tracksuit and red beanie, while he dances to Cabello's crop top, leggings, high ponytail and red sunglasses. View this post on Instagram we had to do it A post shared by camila (@camila_cabello) on Mar 9, 2020 at 12:20pm PDT Elizabeth Warren and Kate McKinnon Elizabeth Warren joined in on the fun with Saturday Night Live star Kate McKinnon, who plays the Massachusetts senator on the sketch comedy show. They take turns awkwardly dancing (looking exactly alike) while the other holds the phone in front of a mirror. Emily Ratajkowski The model called on Josh Ostrovsky aka The Fat Jewish for her video. The two are in the bathroom, as Ratajkowski poses in a crop top and sweatpants. The lights flip, and the two switch clothes, with the Instagram star in EmRata's look and the model in Ostrovsky's Justin Bieber merch and American flag underwear.
